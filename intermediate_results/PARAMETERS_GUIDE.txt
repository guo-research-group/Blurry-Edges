================================================================================
BLURRY-EDGES PARAMETERS GUIDE
================================================================================

WHAT ARE THESE PARAMETERS?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Blurry-Edges uses coded aperture imaging to estimate depth from blur patterns.
The LocalStage CNN extracts 10 parameters per image patch describing the blur.

PARAMETER BREAKDOWN (LocalStage Output):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
File: raw_parameters/imgXXX_params_local.npy
Shape: [2, H_patches, W_patches, 10]

Dimensions:
  [0] = View 1 (first image in stereo pair)
  [1] = View 2 (second image in stereo pair)
  [2,3] = Spatial location (row, column of patch in image grid)
  [4] = 10 parameters per patch:

Parameter indices [0:10]:
  [0:4]  - XY Coordinates: (x1, y1, x2, y2)
           Location of two wedge centers in the blur pattern
